# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis

This project analyzes a pizza sales dataset to discover useful insights about customer ordering patterns, product performance, and overall sales trends. The goal of this analysis is to understand which pizzas sell the most, when customers place the most orders, and how factors like pizza size, category, and month affect total revenue.

The analysis was performed using **Python in google colab**

---

## 📊 Project Overview

In this project, I explored the pizza sales dataset to answer important business questions such as:

- Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
- At what time of the day do most orders occur?
- Which months have the highest sales?
- Which pizza sizes are most popular?
- How do different pizza categories contribute to total sales?

This project demonstrates how data analysis can help businesses understand their performance and make better decisions.

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains information related to pizza orders, including:

- Order ID
- Pizza name
- Pizza category
- Pizza size
- Quantity sold
- Order date and time
- Total price

This information allows us to analyze sales trends and customer behavior.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Top Performing Pizzas
Some pizzas contribute significantly more revenue than others.  
The **Thai Chicken Pizza**, **Barbecue Chicken Pizza**, and **California Chicken Pizza** were among the top revenue-generating pizzas.

### 2. Sales by Time of Day
Most orders occur during the **afternoon**, followed by the **evening**.  
Morning and late-night orders are comparatively lower.

### 3. Monthly Sales Trends
Sales vary across different months.  
**July** recorded the highest sales, while **September and October** showed lower sales compared to other months.

### 4. Pizza Category Performance
The **Classic category** contributes the most to total sales, followed by **Supreme**, **Chicken**, and **Veggie** categories.

### 5. Pizza Size Demand
**Large-sized pizzas** generate the highest revenue compared to Medium and Small sizes.
